WHAT IS AI ?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions similar humans. They cant truly think, but they prescribe actions based on data and algorithms. Basically, they are robotic systems created by humans to learn patterns, make predictions, and automate tasks.

Problems OF AI:

AI faces challenges like Bias, Lack of Transparency, and Hallucinations that can lead to unfair decisions. It also raises concerns about Privacy, Security, and Job displacement in society.
